Cell Stop

Communication // Packaging // Industrial Design

With the evolution of smartphone technology more people have become disconnected with their immediate surroundings and have increasingly become addicted to their phones. Cell Stop allows users to refocus their attention on the moment. Using an electromagnetic mesh lining Cell Stop keeps phone signals from coming in or out. With this pouch users openly choose to make a commitment to what is happening around them. Pouches can also be connected with other Cell Stop pouches during group activities as a way of agreeing to pay more attention to each other, rather than the shiny screen in your pocket.

Cell Stop can be used in a variety of scenarios including: group study, meetings, dates, personal work time, play time for children, etc.
