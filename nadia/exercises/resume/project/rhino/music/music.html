DIE ANTWOORD: HEADPHONES

CAD // Branding // Industrial Design

The design of these headphones was influenced by the South African "rap/rave" group Die Antwoord. Their unique "zef" style is one of the many reasons that they were chosen as a main sourse of inspiration for this product.

These headphones were designed to best fit the unique style of Die Antwoord and their sound. The over the ear style guarantees the best quality sound so you can kick back and enjoy the music.
