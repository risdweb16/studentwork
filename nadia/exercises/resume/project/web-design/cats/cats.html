How Cute

Let me tell you something about the web. It is a beautiful and magical place, where anything can happen, and where a picture of a cat could lead you to 100 different places.

Behind every image is a doorway. This doorway could lead you to the same place as another link or it could be its own unique connection. We could spend hours opening and closing doors, just to see what we could find. And in this spirit I encourage you to explore this web page, not because you know where you will end up but because why not? After all is that not what the internet is all about, the opportunity to explore?
