GET TO KNOW ME
 
 
     

Hey there!

My name is Nadia.

I am a California based artist, designer, & entrepreneur with a background in sculpture , graphic design, & product design.

I am currently finishing up my last semester at the Rhode Island School of Design as an Industrial Designer (class of 2016). The last four years have taught me an endless array of skills that have helped me in producing what my portfolio is today. These projects took blood, sweat, and tears and I would give an arm and a leg too, if only I could regenerate. Oh well . . . maybe in the future?

When I am not working on my own projects I am a monitor for both the Metal Shop and CAD Lab in the Industrial Design Department at RISD. This means I am trusted to watch over and help out other student as they spend any free time they wish they had in the shops. I have also spent a summer as a Ceramics intern for the HAAS Brothers in Los Angeles

It has been a pretty wild ride so far but now I am ready for my next adventure.

So get let's get in touch and make some cool things happen.
