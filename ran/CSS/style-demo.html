Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

The crust:

  • butter - 60g
  • egg yolk - 1
  • sugar - 40g
  • flour - 120g

The filling:

  • heavy cream - 1 cup
  • 2 eggs
  • cream cheese - 1 pack
  • flour - 3 TBSP
  • sugar - 2/3 cup
  • lemon juice - 1TBSP

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Put crust ingredients in food processor
  3. Press into cooking pan
  4. Pour filling onto crust
  5. bake at 350 F for about 30 min. time will depend on oven strength.
