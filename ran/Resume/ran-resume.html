Instead of fawning over musicians, I prefer analyzing the data of their audiences' reaction to their live performance. I prefer to keep up with renovations around the world, such as tech and inventions emerging on Kickstarter. I also like to be hands on when solving problems, like making an alarm system on my bag after losing three cell phones. My more hobby-like passions include traveling, cooking, and some sports. WORK

2013 - 2015 CO-FOUNDER Beijing Shuyintang Sound Data Processing Co., Ltd
As the co-founder and creative director of this company I established it with geek musician Wang Bolong in 2013 which is dedicated to colleting music data. We’ve done some excellent work, such as laying the special carpet in livehouse MAO in Beijing. During the bands playing the carpet can collect different audience’s feet jump frequency and amplitude which could reflect the mood of the audience. Also we made a special wastepaper basket for songwriters which could generate rhyming words from the trash. The company has been reported by Economic Information Daily, Beijing International Design Week and Pingwest, and got investment from the Baidu Cloud Angel funds.
