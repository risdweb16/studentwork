Ingredients

The crust:

  • butter - 60g
  • egg yolk - 1
  • sugar - 40g
  • flour - 120g

The filling:

  • heavy cream - 1 cup
  • 2 eggs
  • cream cheese - 1 pack
  • flour - 3 TBSP
  • sugar - 2/3 cup
  • lemon juice - 1 TBSP

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Put crust ingredients in food processor
  3. Press into cooking pan. Poke a couple holes in the dough with a fork.
  4. Bake for 10 min. Wash food processor in the meantime and blend filling ingredients.
  5. After 10 min, pour filling onto crust.
  6. Bake for about 30 min. Time will depend on oven strength.Check by inserting a toothpick into the cake; theren't should be any goo when pulled out.
